Help for petals around the rose

This is a variation of an old dice game. I'll throw five dice, then ask you "How many petals around the rose?" Type a number into the text box, and press the submit button. The answer is related to the die rolls. There really is a pattern.

Hints:

  • The name of the game is really important
  • Most people think way too hard
  • Use the "back" button to look back over your guesses
  • Did I mention the name means something?
